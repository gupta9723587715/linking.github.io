"The Twelve Ceasers" by Suetonius

Gaius Suetonius Tranquillus was probably born about 69 AD.a date deduced from his remarks describing himself as a "young man" twenty years after Nero's death. His place of birth is disputed, but most scholars place it in Hippo Regius, a small north African town in Numidia, in modern-day Algeria. His work includes biographies of the following roman emperore:

  • Julius Ceasers
  • Augustus
  • Tiberius
  • Caligula
  • Cladius
  • Nero
  • Galba
  • Otho
  • Vitellius
  • Vespasian
  • Titus
  • Domitian
